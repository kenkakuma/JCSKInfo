Whether you’re into shooters, fighting games or immersive RPGs, there’s something for every Xbox owner out there. While Microsoft’s first-party output has been a bit slim lately, the company has been on a tear [acquiring the likes of Bethesda](https://www.engadget.com/microsoft-buys-zenimax-bethesda-id-arkane-131006192.html) and [Activision Blizzard](https://www.engadget.com/activision-blizzard-now-officially-belongs-to-microsoft-125053787.html), as well as pumping up the amount of worthwhile titles in its [Game Pass](https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?merchantId=5f41950e-8ca3-4481-8466-a22b28b80e32&siteId=us-engadget&pageId=1p-autolink&contentUuid=0a6e6edb-4b36-4290-b5e5-a44abb6024c8&featureId=text-link&merchantName=Xbox&linkText=Game+Pass&custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy54Ym94LmNvbS9lbi1VUy94Ym94LWdhbWUtcGFzcyIsImNvbnRlbnRVdWlkIjoiMGE2ZTZlZGItNGIzNi00MjkwLWI1ZTUtYTQ0YWJiNjAyNGM4Iiwib3JpZ2luYWxVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy54Ym94LmNvbS9lbi1VUy94Ym94LWdhbWUtcGFzcyJ9&signature=AQAAAb5xyjhLEmTAZoJBYKGYsCIhCYaV71iHKVGelt-C-Hum&gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.xbox.com%2Fen-US%2Fxbox-game-pass) subscription service. To make life a bit easier, we’ve highlighted some of the best titles available on the Xbox Series X and S (some of which are also playable on older Xbox One systems). We’ve chosen a mix of old and new titles, but given the hundreds of hours we’ve put into these games, we can guarantee they’re well worth your time.
