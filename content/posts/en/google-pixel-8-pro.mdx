## Introduction

The **Google Pixel 8 Pro** showcases Google's AI prowess with the new Tensor G3 chip, groundbreaking camera features, and an industry-leading 7-year update promise.

## Design & Display

### Premium Build

- **Material**: Polished aluminum frame
- **Glass**: Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2
- **Weight**: 213g
- **IP Rating**: IP68
- **Colors**: Bay, Obsidian, Porcelain

### Super Actua Display

- **Size**: 6.7-inch LTPO OLED
- **Resolution**: 2992 x 1344 (489 ppi)
- **Refresh Rate**: 1-120Hz adaptive
- **Brightness**: 2400 nits peak (HDR)
- **Always-On**: Enhanced AOD with customization

## Google Tensor G3

### Performance

- **CPU**: 9-core (1x Cortex-X3 + 4x A715 + 4x A510)
- **GPU**: Mali-G715
- **AI**: Dedicated ML cores
- **Process**: 4nm Samsung
- **RAM**: 12GB LPDDR5X

### AI Capabilities

- On-device machine learning
- Real-time translation
- Advanced photo/video processing
- Voice recognition
- Security features
