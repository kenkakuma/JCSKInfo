Fans of miniature plastic soldiers, rejoice. Games Workshop has brought a host of older Warhammer and Warhammer 40K video games to [Steam for the first time](https://www.warhammer-community.com/en-gb/articles/ud4dbah1/warhammer-classics-brings-legendary-games-back-to-your-pc/), alongside a dozen games that haven't been available on Valve's storefront for a few years. The new to Steam releases consist of three games from the Warhammer fantasy range — [_Shadow of the Horned Rat_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280870/Warhammer_Shadow_of_the_Horned_Rat_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830), [_Mark of Chaos – Gold Edition_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280940/Warhammer_Mark_of_Chaos__Gold_Edition_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830) and [_Dark Omen_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280860/Warhammer_Dark_Omen_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830) — and four from its sci-fi 40K universe — [_Chaos Gate_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280890/Warhammer_40000_Chaos_Gate_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830), [_Fire Warrior_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280930/Warhammer_40000_Fire_Warrior_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830), [_Final Liberation_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280920/Final_Liberation_Warhammer_Epic_40000_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830) and [_Rites of War_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280900/Warhammer_40000_Rites_of_War_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830).  

If you're a Warhammer fan of a certain age, some of these may be formative experiences for you. I know they are for me. I can't count how many hours I spent playing _Chaos Gate_ when I first discovered 40K at the age of 10. Yes, it was an _XCOM_ clone, but by that point I didn't know about the MicroProse original, and Space Marines were cool. 

Years later and as a Tau collector at the time, I also loved _Fire Warrior,_ even if it wasn't the most polished or deep first-person shooter. I haven't played the other five games included in today's announcement, but I've heard _Warhammer: Shadow of the Horned Rat_ and _Warhammer 40K: Rites of War_ are pretty good if you're into the setting or, in the latter case, a fan of the Eldar.  

To celebrate the re-release of these old gems, Games Workshop is running a [Classics sale on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/sale/warhammer-classics), with discounts on all 19 re-releases. Plus, you can get discounts on some more recent releases, including the excellent [_Dawn of War – Definitive Edition_ and _Dawn of War 2 – Anniversary Edition_](https://www.engadget.com/gaming/boltgun--words-of-vengeance-is-warhammers-grimdark-answer-to-typing-of-the-dead-193515536.html)_._ If you're new to the Warhammer 40K universe, and would rather avoid a plastic addiction, one of those would be my first port of call, along with the excellent [_Space Marine 2_](https://www.engadget.com/gaming/warhammer-40k-space-marine-2-is-unsurprisingly-getting-a-sequel-200055031.html).
