[Bixby](https://www.engadget.com/2020-01-23-samsung-bixby-smart-home-smartthings.html) isn’t typically part of the conversation when it comes to virtual assistants for mobile devices, but Samsung is clearly hoping that you would use it more. The company has [launched](https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?siteId=us-engadget&pageId=1p-autolink&contentUuid=dcb4fd75-9f68-41a1-94f6-e090ec11b1f7&featureId=text-link&linkText=launched&custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL25ld3Muc2Ftc3VuZy5jb20vZ2xvYmFsL3NhbXN1bmctaW50cm9kdWNlcy10aGUtbmV3LWJpeGJ5LWluLW9uZS11aS04LTUiLCJjb250ZW50VXVpZCI6ImRjYjRmZDc1LTlmNjgtNDFhMS05NGY2LWUwOTBlYzExYjFmNyIsIm9yaWdpbmFsVXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9uZXdzLnNhbXN1bmcuY29tL2dsb2JhbC9zYW1zdW5nLWludHJvZHVjZXMtdGhlLW5ldy1iaXhieS1pbi1vbmUtdWktOC01In0&signature=AQAAATWMjHIHQUXAAZyKXvCBOcDHuuNXl-O-8VG-qMOYB0ti&gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fnews.samsung.com%2Fglobal%2Fsamsung-introduces-the-new-bixby-in-one-ui-8-5) the latest version of Bixby with the new One UI 8.5 beta, and it has been tweaked to work as a “conversational agent.” Samsung says you’ll now be able to talk to it and give it tasks using natural language, like how you’d talk to other people or, these days, to chatbots.

You don’t have to remember exact commands or names for specific settings. You can just describe what you want to happen, such as “I don’t want the screen to time out while I’m still looking at it.” Bixby will then automatically turn on the “Keep Screen on While Viewing” setting. If you ask it a question, such as “Why is my phone screen always on when it’s inside my pocket,” it could provide several solutions you can choose from.

In addition, the assistant can now access new and up-to-date information on the web. You do searches without opening a browser, and Bixby will display web results right within its interface. At the moment, the updated Bixby is only available in Samsung’s home country of Korea, as well as in Germany, India, Poland, the UK and the US, but company will roll it out more widely in the future.
