[Toy Fair](https://toyfairny.com/) 2026 just wrapped earlier this week and while I would have liked to spend even more time there, I have my own kids (and all their toys and trinkets) to look after. That said, there were a ton of cool new products on display at the Javits Center in New York City that set the stage for the rest of the year, so here's a quick look at some of the most interesting releases from the largest toy show in the Western Hemisphere. 

## [_Transformers: The Movie_ 40th anniversary figures](https://www.hasbropulse.com/collections/the-transformers-the-movie) ($28 to $60)

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of _Transformers: The Movie_, Hasbro is launching an [apology tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3U62UyYw7ek) to make up for traumatizing theatergoers with the death of the most beloved Autobot back in 1986. To kick things off, Hasbro is releasing a handful of new figures alongside re-releases for some popular bots including [Astrotrain](https://www.hasbropulse.com/product/transformers-studio-series-the-transformers-the-movie-leader-class-astrotrain/G21905X00?srsltid=AfmBOoqfDHC5xVvWabTc6P-MfWUbaUkgH8TozCODjikqwPM-agrTAHrB), [Skywarp](https://www.hasbropulse.com/product/transformers-studio-series-the-transformers-the-movie-voyager-class-skywarp/G19315X00?srsltid=AfmBOorJcUnsU1uK8BaUEnGKRoN7gCwBY6ArYBsccQtCQXJhl5z7bzE6), [Snarl](https://www.hasbropulse.com/product/transformers-studio-series-the-transformers-the-movie-leader-class-dinobot-snarl/G21885X00?srsltid=AfmBOopQzlhPkzfCcba4nQnstDA-lcJueg0JY52RBm5Sh9cMatsQIUg9) and [Shockwave](https://www.hasbropulse.com/product/transformers-studio-series-the-transformers-the-movie-voyager-class-shockwave/G21915X00?cgid=the-transformers-the-movie). I want to give a special shout-out to the model for [Kranix](https://www.hasbropulse.com/product/transformers-studio-series-the-transformers-the-movie-deluxe-class-kranix/G21955X00?srsltid=AfmBOoqlkWRUIbY6Hix8oQ3uwvhhhr-cvycKdntHIBsCRUCeR79sJyul), which looks incredibly accurate, as if he just leapt off the movie screen. And even though his duck-billed spaceship alt-mode might look a bit awkward, I wouldn't have it any other way. 

The crown jewel of the line might be a near-life-size version of The Matrix of Leadership, which measures more than 15 inches wide and even plays Stan Bush's iconic song "The Touch" with the push of a button. Unfortunately, the appeal of the Matrix is so powerful that it's already sold out, including at third-party retailers like [Big Bad Toy Store](https://www.bigbadtoystore.com/Search?HideSoldOut=true&PageSize=100&SortOrder=New&Series=13851), which thankfully is still taking pre-orders for the rest of the lineup after the initial stock from Hasbro dried up.

![A collection of some of the new F1 Hot Wheels cars for 2026. ](https://d29szjachogqwa.cloudfront.net/images/user-uploaded/f1-hotwheels.jpg)

A collection of some of the new F1 Hot Wheels cars for 2026.

Sam Rutherford for Engadget

Hot Wheels has big plans for 2026 including a new line of [Pantone-colored cars](https://wheelcollectors.com/products/pantone-complete-6-car-set-2026-hot-wheels-pantone-silver-series-case-a), Brick Shop models like the [Elite Series Aston Martin](https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?merchantId=3719d8d4-5edd-4817-998a-91f3229e7323&itemId=walmart_transaction_18178716480&siteId=us-engadget&pageId=1p-autolink&contentUuid=ef0c566d-d250-409f-9be2-d3e658e794d5&featureId=text-link&merchantName=Walmart&linkText=Elite+Series+Aston+Martin&custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy53YWxtYXJ0LmNvbS9pcC9IVy1FbGl0ZS1Bc3Rvbi8xODE3ODcxNjQ4MCIsImNvbnRlbnRVdWlkIjoiZWYwYzU2NmQtZDI1MC00MDlmLTliZTItZDNlNjU4ZTc5NGQ1Iiwib3JpZ2luYWxVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy53YWxtYXJ0LmNvbS9pcC9IVy1FbGl0ZS1Bc3Rvbi8xODE3ODcxNjQ4MCJ9&signature=AQAAAfVUAAnzBkVHUeyUzBLjhRKnBGaLIokDjzyS7h1kDCrX&gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.walmart.com%2Fip%2FHW-Elite-Aston%2F18178716480) (which comes with its own 1:64 scale car) and a [Monster Truck Mutant Chaos set with actual slime](https://thetoyinsider.com/hot-wheels-news/). However, I'd argue the company's new [F1 offerings](https://shop.mattel.com/pages/hot-wheels-f1) are the cream of the crop. Not only are there a bunch of incredibly detailed 1:64 scale racecars with metal bodies, real rubber tires and accurate livery for all the big teams, there's also a new Downhill Circuit Race course that comes with three official vehicles (Mercedes, Haas and Ferrari) featuring multiple levels and the ability to overtake or crash into other cars. If you're like a lot of Americans who have recently fallen down the F1 rabbit hole due to Netflix's _Drive to Survive_, these new officially licensed miniatures are sure to hit the spot. 

The first [five-pack set of cars](https://shop.mattel.com/products/hot-wheels-5-pack-race-team-formula-1-themed-cars-jgf58) is available now, with more arriving later this spring before the Downhill Circuit Race course drives by sometime this fall.

## All the new K-pop Demon Hunters toys

Rumi, Mira and Zoey may have been the biggest breakout stars of 2025 and Mattel is looking to keep that momentum going with a ton of new toys and figures for everyone's favorite demon hunters. There are three new singing dolls that can belt out the trio's hit "Golden" at the touch of a button and a deluxe figure of Rumi complete with her Four Tiger Sword. There are also a ton of other dolls and miniatures showcasing HUNTR/X, the Saja Boys and more. The one downside is that these products aren't coming out until the fall, so you'll have to tide yourself over with other K-pop-themed products for now.

## [Thames and Kosmos SolarFlowers](https://store.thamesandkosmos.com/collections/solarflowers?srsltid=AfmBOorEeTX5jzBGX1-HpFD8dsT2n2UX8WcRqBdWRNKrA3sALWjBCQKy)

Technically, these went on sale last month, but Thames & Kosmos' SolarFlowers caught my eye again at Toy Fair due to their combination of art and science. Available in four different styles, each kit features a model that you can build yourself or with your kids (recommended age 8+) that turns into a lasting showpiece. After putting the kinetic sculpture together, you can connect the included solar panel to bring the whole kit to life (no batteries required) and make the flowers spin for perpetual entertainment.
