At GTC 2026, NVIDIA and Bolt announced what they hope will be a symbiotic partnership. Bolt gets NVIDIA technology that would be costly and impractical to build on its own. Meanwhile, NVIDIA not only gains a major customer but also access to the European rideshare company’s driving data.

Bolt says its fleet data will build a "learning engine" for autonomous vehicles (AVs) using NVIDIA tech. The rideshare company will use [NVIDIA Cosmos](https://www.engadget.com/ai/nvidia-ceo-jensen-huang-welcomes-the-rise-of-superintelligent-ai-at-ces-2025-002827074.html) to curate and search driving data. It will tap into [NVIDIA Omniverse](https://www.engadget.com/nvidia-ai-voice-avatar-omniverse-project-tokkio-142456487.html/1000) to reconstruct digital twins of real-world driving logs, then use Cosmos again to generate and augment data at scale.

NVIDIA's [Alpamayo model](https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?merchantId=f52348f6-2304-415e-b16c-1fb252794066&siteId=us-engadget&pageId=1p-autolink&contentUuid=0d967013-f22e-4eab-8b8e-5bd9867a0bc0&featureId=text-link&merchantName=NVIDIA&linkText=Alpamayo+model&custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy5udmlkaWEuY29tL2VuLXVzL3NvbHV0aW9ucy9hdXRvbm9tb3VzLXZlaGljbGVzL2FscGFtYXlvLyIsImNvbnRlbnRVdWlkIjoiMGQ5NjcwMTMtZjIyZS00ZWFiLThiOGUtNWJkOTg2N2EwYmMwIiwib3JpZ2luYWxVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy5udmlkaWEuY29tL2VuLXVzL3NvbHV0aW9ucy9hdXRvbm9tb3VzLXZlaGljbGVzL2FscGFtYXlvLyJ9&signature=AQAAATU2HnF4qlOVFztE6RygEO-fQe2WboQJ8X3NmwuqEzvz&gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.nvidia.com%2Fen-us%2Fsolutions%2Fautonomous-vehicles%2Falpamayo%2F), designed specifically for AVs, will help the AI learn how to drive safely and appropriately in European cities. Finally, Bolt will integrate [NVIDIA's Drive Hyperion platform](https://www.engadget.com/ai/everything-nvidia-ceo-jensen-huang-announced-at-its-ces-2025-keynote-174947827.html) into its AVs.

"Autonomous vehicles require a full-stack approach that unifies AI models, high-performance compute, and a robust sensor architecture," NVIDIA EMEA Automotive VP Philippe Van Den Berge said. "By combining Bolt's real-world operational data with the NVIDIA Drive Hyperion platform, AI infrastructure, and open models & libraries across Omniverse, Cosmos, and Alpamayo, we're enabling a scalable foundation for safe, high-performance autonomous mobility services designed for the complexity and diversity of European roads."

Bolt has been busy gearing up for an autonomous future. In late 2025, it announced partnerships with [Pony.ai](https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?merchantId=678df671-e59c-4645-9fb1-68c12b7b2247&siteId=us-engadget&pageId=1p-autolink&contentUuid=0d967013-f22e-4eab-8b8e-5bd9867a0bc0&featureId=text-link&merchantName=Bolt&linkText=Pony.ai&custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL2JvbHQuZXUvZW4vYmxvZy9ib2x0LXBvbnlhaS1hdXRvbm9tb3VzLWRyaXZpbmctZXVyb3BlLyIsImNvbnRlbnRVdWlkIjoiMGQ5NjcwMTMtZjIyZS00ZWFiLThiOGUtNWJkOTg2N2EwYmMwIiwib3JpZ2luYWxVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL2JvbHQuZXUvZW4vYmxvZy9ib2x0LXBvbnlhaS1hdXRvbm9tb3VzLWRyaXZpbmctZXVyb3BlLyJ9&signature=AQAAAetOLI4ooD0NJqDuM3G9tG6lN-0GpHczfkW7c9hoxZ33&gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fbolt.eu%2Fen%2Fblog%2Fbolt-ponyai-autonomous-driving-europe%2F) and [Stellantis](https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?merchantId=678df671-e59c-4645-9fb1-68c12b7b2247&siteId=us-engadget&pageId=1p-autolink&contentUuid=0d967013-f22e-4eab-8b8e-5bd9867a0bc0&featureId=text-link&merchantName=Bolt&linkText=Stellantis&custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL2JvbHQuZXUvZW4vYmxvZy9zdGVsbGFudGlzLWJvbHQtcHJlc3MtcmVsZWFzZS8iLCJjb250ZW50VXVpZCI6IjBkOTY3MDEzLWYyMmUtNGVhYi04YjhlLTViZDk4NjdhMGJjMCIsIm9yaWdpbmFsVXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9ib2x0LmV1L2VuL2Jsb2cvc3RlbGxhbnRpcy1ib2x0LXByZXNzLXJlbGVhc2UvIn0&signature=AQAAAZHyD9pbgn80YWYKEztUcp_73iEgEXmO7cSG-2sYSLit&gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fbolt.eu%2Fen%2Fblog%2Fstellantis-bolt-press-release%2F).

The companies haven't announced a timeline for when we can expect to see NVIDIA-powered Bolt robotaxis in European cities. However, they promise that Bolt's fleet data will comply with [GDPR](https://www.engadget.com/big-tech/european-policymakers-want-to-ease-ai-and-privacy-laws-171118149.html) standards. They also say they’ll provide open-source access to European universities and small- and medium-sized businesses.
