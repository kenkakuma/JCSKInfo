Sony and Polyphony Digital are rolling out a huge update for [_Gran Turismo 7_](https://www.engadget.com/gran-tourismo-7s-more-realistic-physics-update-is-launching-cars-into-orbit-120045578.html) on December 4 in the form of a paid add-on. The [Power Pack DLC](https://blog.playstation.com/2025/11/11/gran-turismo-7-power-pack-dlc-coming-december-4/) for the PlayStation 5 will unlock 50 new races across 20 theme categories, including 24-hour endurance events that Le Mans fans could sink their teeth into. In previous games, the endurance events really took 24 hours to finish, though you could pause and, eventually, save mid-race, allowing you to pick up where you left off. You can also experience full-race seasons, from practicing for them to qualifying for the main events.
